Dessert

This decadent dessert features a warm, fudgy chocolate brownie topped with a perfectly rounded scoop of velvety vanilla ice cream. A crisp, striped wafer roll is perched across the top, adding a light crunch to the rich base. The entire sundae is being drizzled with a thick, golden caramel sauce that flows over ribbons of dark chocolate syrup, creating a beautiful contrast of textures and temperatures against the moody, dark background.
